# Placeholders
- `%psextras_current_name%` - The name of the current region the player is in.
- `%psextras_current_world%` - The world name of the current region the player is in. 
- `%psextras_current_owners%` - The owners of the current region the player is in, separated by commas.
- `%psextras_owner%` - The owner of the region (useful in region management GUI).
- `%psextras_member%` - The member of the region (useful in region management GUI).
- `%psextras_banned%` - The banned players of the region (useful in region management GUI).
- `%psextras_x%` - The X coordinate of the region (useful in region management GUI).
- `%psextras_y%` - The Y coordinate of the region (useful in region management GUI).
- `%psextras_z%` - The Z coordinate of the region (useful in region management GUI).
