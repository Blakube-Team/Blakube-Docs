# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: January 1, 2025*

At Blakube Design, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it and your rights regarding your data.

## Information we collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our services:

- Username or alias used on platforms (Spigot, Modrinth, BuiltByBit)
- Discord username when you contact our support team
- Purchase information processed by third-party platforms
- Server information voluntarily shared for support purposes

## How we use your information

Any information collected is used exclusively to provide support, process purchases and improve our products. We do not sell, share or transfer your personal data to third parties.

## Third-party platforms

Our plugins are distributed through Spigot, Modrinth and BuiltByBit. Purchases and payments are handled entirely by these platforms under their own privacy policies. We do not store payment information.

## Cookies and tracking

This documentation site does not use tracking cookies or analytics that identify individual users.

## Data retention

Support conversations are retained only as long as necessary to resolve the issue. You may request deletion of any personal data by contacting us on Discord.

## Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach us at [discord.blakube.com](https://discord.blakube.com).
