# Introduction
Welcome to the **SH-Pets** documentation.

## About SH-Pets
SH-Pets is a **premium** plugin for interactive pets. It is designed to be simple to configure,
yet powerful in action. It provides a smarter way to manage collectible and functional pets on your server,
with features that enhance gameplay and improve player retention.

The main purpose of SH-Pets is to offer a reliable and feature-rich solution for hosting interactive pets on your server.

### Some of the features include:
- **Unleash Fully Customizable Pet Interactions:** Bring your worlds alive. Make pets run commands, send messages, play sounds, and build manageable bonds with players. **Includes over 15 types of visual attacks for abilities.**
- **Choose from Custom Heads & Mobs:** Summon pets using custom heads or any Minecraft mob, including adorable baby versions!
- **Dynamic, Event-Based Triggers:** Create truly interactive experiences. Link pet actions to server events like periodic buffs, server rewards, or special moments.
- **Stunning Particle Effects & Animations:** Turn interactions into a visual spectacle. Choose from dazzling particle trails, bursting effects, or sparkles that follow the pet.
- **No-Code Setup, Total Power:** Get started in minutes. Simple configuration files let you design complex pet behaviors with zero coding required.
- **Boost Player Engagement & Retention:** Players adore collecting custom pets. Turn that love into long-term retention. Create collectible series and cosmetic upgrades.
- **Seamless Integration:** Full PlaceholderAPI and MiniMessage support. Give your server modern text with customizable hover effects and clickable text.
- **Professional Support & Community-Driven Updates:** Join a growing community. Enjoy friendly support and consistent updates for this premium plugin.
